ABOUT US

LED TVs are more energy-efficient as these models use light-emitting diodes (LED) for backlighting. These TVs consume less power as compared to Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamps (CCFL), which most LCD TVs use.

You won't get the same excellent dark room picture quality as an OLED, but LED TVs have a few advantages compared to OLEDs (check out our article about OLED vs. LED). LED TVs' biggest advantage is their brightness, which allows them to get brighter than OLEDs, and they're immune to permanent burn-in.
